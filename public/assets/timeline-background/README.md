# Timeline Background Asset

The timeline background uses one custom colour image:

- `timeline-background-long.jpg`

Current size:

- `2560 x 7200 px`

The image is rendered behind the introduction and research timeline, keeps its
original colour, scrolls upward with the timeline, and fades out before the
collective 3D scene becomes interactive.

Replacement guidance:

- Keep the filename stable.
- Use a tall image around `2560 x 7200 px`.
- Keep important details inside the centre `1600 px`.
- Leave the final lower section visually quieter so the opacity fade into the
  3D scene feels clean.
